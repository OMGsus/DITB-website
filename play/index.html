Rules

Our rules are simple.
-Do not discourage other players
-Do not cheat or use "hacks"
-Be nice and use your manners
-Be a good sport

Over all, use common sense and have manners. We're all in to have fun! Rule breakers will be kicked/banned for as long as a moderator desires.

Is there a bad player? Report them using the /report [username] [reason] command.

How do you run the games?

You guessed it, how we run our games is simple:

-You enter and if all games are running you'll wait for a spot
-After playing a game, you and the other players get to vote on what map to play next
-You're entered into the next map and you play a round
