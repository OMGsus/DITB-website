DANK IN THE BANK

24/7 Minecraft Server

What's our goal?

We are (Greendale) human beings that want to provide a friendly, fun, and small-scale Minecraft server to the public. Not only are we a 24/7 open Minecraft server, but we're also completely open to suggestions and help from strangers. If you would like to contribute, you can build us a map, revise and improve our code (which is open source), or suggest anything else.
