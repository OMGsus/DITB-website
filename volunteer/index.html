VOLUNTEER

Why not use your free-time wisely?

What can I help with?

Dank in the Bank (DITB) strongly believes in help from strangers to make the experience better for everyone. You can (click on any of them for more info):
-Make a pull request to our open source plugins
-Build a map for us
-Suggest an idea
-Make a pull request to make our website better

Am I missing something? Email me: contact@edwinfinch.com.

What process do my works go through?

For maps:
-You submit to contact@edwinfinch.com in the form of a ZIP file.
-Your map gets reviewed by team (not judged, this is to make sure it's appropriate)
-Your map gets voted on at www.dankinthebank.com/vote/
-The winning map gets a spot on the server for at least 3 weeks and can be voted back in if people really like it.

For code:
-Edwin reviews your changes
-If they're worth putting in, they will be tested
-If they work well they will be implemented in the server/site.

Suggestions:
-They're reviewed by the team
-Voted on by the team
-Applied if it's worth it

Submit

Submitting is simple:

Maps:
-Send your map in a ZIP file to contact@edwinfinch.com

Code:
-Make a commit on Github for either the site or plugins
-Pull the commit to master branch

Suggestions:
-Post on our suggestions section: here.
